What is the MA4T9?

A simple & powerful data processor. The program was built in-house by PeakLab and is rooted on our negative-neutral choice method. The dynamic experience offers a "no wrong answers" approach, and was designed to only render positive results, for all users. Our algorythm will compare, measure and analyze your metrics with thousands of other users. The MA4T9 can analyze 25,827,165 diferent outcomes out of 6 selections to be made.
